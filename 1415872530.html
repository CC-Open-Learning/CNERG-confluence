[Key Points]

  1. Device ‘Foot Traffic Counter’ Management:

    1. The device should always be attended for two main reasons:

      1. Accuracy Validation: To verify how accurately the scanner detects people.

      2. Theft and Loss Prevention: The device must remain supervised to minimize the risk of theft or loss.

  2. Data Validation and Testing:

    1. Testing is required for scenarios where two people walk very closely together.

    2. Currently, the device only collects IN/OUT data without tracking individual movements or locations.

    3. There is a consideration for future scenarios like tracking the number of people remaining in a building during an evacuation.

  3. 3D Modeling and Unity Integration:

    1. Plans to integrate building floor plans into Unity for simulation purposes.

  4. Heuristic NPC:

    1. Use heuristic NPC to simulate human movement patterns.

  5. Security and Data Transmission:

    1. In the initial phase (this semester), data transmission will be wired only to avoid cybersecurity assessments.

  6. Dashboard Development:

    1. The team is working on creating dashboard prototypes that visualize data pulled from the server.

    2. The goal is to test how these graphs display on the PC and the Tilt Five platform.

    3. Depending on the results of the prototype, they will decide whether to continue with the current approach or adjust it.
