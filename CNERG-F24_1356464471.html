Meet Our Team!

We're a group of passionate individuals working on the Smart Campus Digital Twin project, and we're excited to make a difference on our college campus. Our goal is to collect real-time foot traffic data using motion sensors placed throughout the campus. By analyzing this data, we hope to uncover insights that can help improve the use of our infrastructure.
